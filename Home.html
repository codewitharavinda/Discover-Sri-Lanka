Why visit Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka packs a remarkable variety of experiences into a small island — ancient ruins, lush tea plantations, wildlife-rich national parks, and golden beaches.

Top Attractions

  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress — climb the Lion Rock for spectacular views.
  • Kandy and the Temple of the Tooth — a cultural heartland.
  • Ella's tea terraces — scenic hiking and train journeys.
  • Yala National Park — spot leopards and elephants.

Travel tips

  1. Pack light layers — weather varies across regions.
  2. Respect local customs in temples (cover shoulders and legs).
  3. Try local transport like the hill country train for unforgettable views.

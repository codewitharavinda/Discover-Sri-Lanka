About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean with a rich history, vibrant culture and stunning landscapes — from highland tea estates to golden beaches.

Highlights: Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Kandy Temple, Yala National Park, Coastal Surfing.

Getting Around

Public buses and trains connect major towns. Renting a car with a driver is common for flexible itineraries. Domestic flights link farther destinations.

Suggested 7-day itinerary

  1. Day 1-2: Colombo and Negombo — beaches and city sights.
  2. Day 3: Kandy — Temple of the Tooth, cultural shows.
  3. Day 4-5: Ella — train journey, tea plantations.
  4. Day 6: Yala National Park — safari for wildlife spotting.
  5. Day 7: Galle Fort and southern beaches.

Practical tips

  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR).
  • Power sockets: Type G (3 rectangular pins) — bring an adapter.
  • Language: Sinhala and Tamil are official; English is widely used in tourism.

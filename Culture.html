Overview

Sri Lankan culture blends Buddhist, Hindu, Islamic and Christian traditions. The island's history stretches back millennia, visible in monasteries, palaces and folklore.

Festivals

  • Vesak: Celebrates the birth, enlightenment and passing of the Buddha with lanterns and decorations.
  • Thai Pongal: A harvest festival celebrated mainly by Tamil communities.
  • Esala Perahera: The grand pageant in Kandy featuring dancers, drummers and decorated elephants.

Traditional Arts

Mask carving, Kandyan dance, batik textiles, and wood carving are celebrated crafts. Temple rituals and classical music remain an important part of cultural life.

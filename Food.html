Must-try dishes

  • Rice and curry — staple meal with several vegetable and meat curries.
  • Hoppers (appa) — bowl-shaped pancakes often served with egg.
  • Pol sambol — spicy coconut relish that accompanies many dishes.
  • String hoppers (idiappam) — steamed rice noodle discs.

Street food & drinks

Try kottu roti from roadside stalls and king coconut water by the beach. Ceylon tea is a highlight — visit a tea estate for a tasting.
